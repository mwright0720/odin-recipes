Pizza

Description:

I like pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza
  • More cheese
  • Maybe some french fries if you're REALLY hungry

Steps:

  1. Get the pizza
  2. If the pizza is not already cooked, then put it in the cooking device of choice
  3. Make sure to take the pizza out of the box if you cook it or else you will have a fire which isn't good
  4. On the cooked pizza, put your extra cheese or french fries if you feel the pizza needs it
  5. Make sure to eat the crust it is there for a reason
  6. Delicious!
